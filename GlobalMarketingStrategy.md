# Whisker Tales: Global Marketing & Budget Optimization Strategy

## 1. Executive Summary
'Whisker Tales' (냥이의 집) is a global match-3 puzzle game designed to captivate a broad audience, primarily focusing on young women and cat lovers, while remaining accessible to older generations. Our marketing strategy centers on high-impact, emotional storytelling through "The Nyang Silence" campaign.

## 2. Core Campaign: "The Nyang Silence"
This campaign highlights the universal appeal of cats as a source of peace in absurd or high-pressure situations.

| Episode | Setting | The "Silence" Moment | Viral Hook |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Frontline** | A gritty, realistic trench during a conflict. | A "Meow?" sound from both sides' phones. | Anti-war sentiment, universal humanity. |
| **The Billionaire's Party** | A high-stakes, formal gala for the elite. | A "Meow?" from the most serious guest's pocket. | Relatability of the "cat butler" lifestyle. |
| **The Peace Summit** | A tense diplomatic negotiation between nations. | A "Purr..." sound during a critical signing. | Humor and tension breaking. |

## 3. Target Audience & Regional Strategy

| Target Group | Key Appeal | Marketing Channel |

### Regional Nuances:
*   **North America/Europe:** Focus on "Premium Healing" and high-quality IAP.
*   **India/Middle East:** Focus on "Universal Peace" and high-volume ad-supported growth.

## 4. Flexible Budget Optimization ($500+ Test & Scale)
We employ a "Test & Scale" approach to maximize ROI.

1.  **Phase 1: Alpha Testing ($500)**
    *   Split $500 across 3 "Nyang Silence" variants on TikTok/Meta.
    *   KPIs: CPI (Cost Per Install), D1 Retention, CTR (Click-Through Rate).
2.  **Phase 2: Data Analysis**
    *   Identify the highest-performing creative and region.
    *   Analyze where users spend (IAP vs. Ads).
3.  **Phase 3: Aggressive Scaling (Additional Budget)**
    *   Inject the user's additional budget into the winning creative/region combination.
    *   Implement "Lookalike" audiences based on high-value "cat butler" users.

## 5. Viral Mechanisms
*   **Signature Notification:** Users can download cat sounds for their messenger apps, turning every notification into a "Whisker Tales" ad.
*   **SNS Sharing:** "Shrek Eyes" request screens and custom "Nyang Club" gardens are designed for high shareability.
